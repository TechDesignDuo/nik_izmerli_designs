Omakase Izakaya


Omakase: お任せ “I leave it up to you”. Often used in Japan, especially in reference to small restaurants – “Izakayas” 居酒屋 – when ordering without a menu and simply trusting the Chef’s choice and experience.

Omakase Izakaya is a popular, upscale restaurant, located in the heart of Montreal. Its fresh flavours, authentic techniques, and trendy atmosphere keep the house packed every night! The only problem...? I made it up.

The Guest Experience.


The lights are dimmed. Not too dark; we want you to look around and notice the carefully selected décor. Not too bright; we want you to feel an intimate ambiance embracing the company you brought with you today.

Our hostess greets you with a warm smile, a friendly demeanor and the kind of hospitality you’d expect when you visit an old friend. From the moment that you’re sat at your table, your server has only 30 seconds to bring you glasses, water and a menu. We want you to know that we’re aware of your presence – we want you to feel welcome.

The menus at your table are closed, a sign for your server that you’ve decided. You took her advice and ordered tonight’s special, excellent choice. We designed the dish specifically for a chilly summer night like this. Crispy pork belly, sautéed shiitake mushrooms, garlic roasted potatoes, pickled Romanesco and carefully reduced demi-glace to help your palette cut through the rich fattiness in the pork. It’s going to pair beautifully with that Pinot Noir you’re enjoying.

Bon appétit! You’ve loved it all: the service, the interior, the food, the drink and the experience.
