I Love You... to Death


Whether it’s the 1981 original or the 2013 – absolutely terrifying – remake, Evil Dead has always been my favorite horror flick. Of course, I just had to make a poster of my own, to represent this timeless classic. Inspired by the creepy cabin in the woods and by the demons in us all, this homage was created with the help of Adobe Illustrator and finished in Adobe Photoshop.

Who's laughing now?!?!

Created in 2D with Adobe Illustrator


The blood Moon can only signify imminent Doom for the poor fool that wandered into these parts. Meanwhile, the creepy old cabin stays bright and warm, heating itself from the burning ashes of miserable souls. The irradiating light casts no doubt about the ill-intent of the demonic, little, undead girl in the doorway.

Enhanced with Adobe Photoshop


The move title, of course, was very fun to do – I always wanted to use the “Shlop” typeface. To provide a more dynamic environment, I added a smoke screen over the illustration to give a bit more life to the… undead.

Undead Experience for the Living Eye


The poster was created using principles of the Fibonacci sequence. Designed to attract the viewers' eyes to the BIG OBVIOUS title, then up towards the smoke and finally down to the centre... towards the little demon in the doorway.
